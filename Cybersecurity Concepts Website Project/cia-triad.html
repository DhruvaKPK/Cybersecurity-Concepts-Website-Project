The CIA Triad

The CIA Triad is a fundamental model in cybersecurity, representing three core principles:

  • Confidentiality: Ensuring sensitive information is accessed only by authorized users.
  • Integrity: Protecting data from being altered or tampered with.
  • Availability: Ensuring information is accessible to authorized users when needed.
