Common Cyber Threats

Be aware of the following potential threats to your cybersecurity:

  • Malware: Malicious software that can damage or disrupt your computer.
  • Ransomware: A type of malware that encrypts your files, demanding payment for access.
  • Phishing: Deceptive attempts to acquire sensitive information.
  • Denial-of-Service Attacks: Attempts to make a service unavailable by overwhelming it with traffic.
