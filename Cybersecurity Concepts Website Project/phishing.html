Understanding Phishing

Phishing is a method used by cybercriminals to trick individuals into providing sensitive information. Here’s how to recognize and avoid phishing attempts:

  • Be cautious of emails or messages that ask for personal information.
  • Look for misspellings or suspicious links in emails.
  • Verify the sender's email address before clicking on links.
  • Use security software that can help detect phishing attempts.
